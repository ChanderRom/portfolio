Who Am I?

My name is Alexander Romero and I'm a hardworking and inquisitive person with a great passion for programming and design. For me technology and programming are indispensable tools for every aspect of my profressional life.

Projects

Kind Time

One of the finalists project of the Caixabank Tech F5 Hackaton. A simple but useful marketplace made in 48h with coders from around Spain. Our main goal was to create a single web application that connects associations, nursing homes or relatives from elder people with volunteers who offer their time to help this group, either by keeping them company or even taking them to the doctor.

Technologies & Methodologies:

-Laravel and PHP for the Back-End
-React, TypeScript and Tailwind for the Front-End
-Scrum, kanban and agile methodologies

Enlace Urbano

Enlace Urbano is a customized, adaptable and scalable CMS made for the NGO with the same name. The project was done in 3 one-week sprints focusing on security and a good UX/UI design while using good practices and agile methodologies such as SCRUM or kanban. During this project I had the chance to develop my skills in both front-end (React, Styled Component, GSAP) and specially back-end (NestJs, MongoDB). TypeScript was used in both stacks.

Technologies & Methodologies:

-NestJS and MongoDB for the Back-End
-React and TypeScript for the Front-End
-Scrum, kanban and agile methodologies
