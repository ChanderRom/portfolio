Who Am I?

My name is Alexander Romero and I'm a hardworking and inquisitive person with a great passion for programming and design. For me technology and programming are indispensable tools for every aspect of my profressional life.

Projects

Kind Time

One of the finalists project of the F5 Hackaton. A simple but useful marketplace made in 48h with coders from around Spain. Our main goal was to create a single web application that connects associations, nursing homes or relatives from elder people with volunteers who offer their time to help this group, either by keeping them company or even taking them to the doctor.

Technologies & Methodologies:

-Laravel and PHP for the Back-End
-React, TypeScript and Tailwind for the Front-End
-Scrum, kanban and agile methodologies

Recette Social

A recipe app for posting and sharing different recipes with the other users. The application was done in two sprints of one week, with the implementation of the database and the possibility to register and logging in.

Technologies & Methodologies:

-NestJS and MongoDB for the Back-End
-React and TypeScript for the Front-End
-Scrum, kanban and agile methodologies
